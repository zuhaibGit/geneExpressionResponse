The files in this directory are labelled with prefix A-F, where the letter represents the group that's being compared to the rest of teh groups
For example, D corresponds to Cov1-Cov2. These are the genes that have a different expression pattern to MERS-Mock1-Mock2.
A: Cov1
B: Cov2
C: MERS
D: Cov1-Cov2
E: Cov1-MERS
F: Cov2-MERS
G: Cov1-Cov2-MERS
